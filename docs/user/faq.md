## Getting Started

### 1. What is Nova Rewards?

Nova Rewards is a loyalty platform that lets you earn digital reward points every time you shop with participating merchants. Your points are stored securely and can be redeemed for discounts, vouchers, and other perks — all in one place.

### 2. How do I create an account?

Click **Sign Up** on the homepage and fill in your name, email address, and a password (at least 8 characters). Once you submit, your account is created instantly and you can start earning points right away.

### 3. Do I need to verify my identity (KYC)?

For basic earning and redemption, no identity verification is required. If you want to withdraw rewards above a certain threshold or access advanced features, we may ask you to complete a quick identity check. This is a standard security step — you'll be guided through it step by step inside the app.

### 4. Is Nova Rewards free to use?

Yes. Creating an account and earning points costs nothing. Some premium redemption options may have a minimum point requirement, but there are no subscription fees.

### 5. What devices and browsers are supported?

Nova Rewards works in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). A mobile-friendly version is available at the same web address — no app download needed.

### 6. I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click **Forgot Password** and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.

## Points & Rewards

### 7. How do I earn points?

Points are awarded automatically when a participating merchant sends you a reward after a qualifying action — such as making a purchase, completing a referral, or taking part in a campaign. The merchant sets the reward rate for each campaign.

### 8. How many points will I earn per purchase?

Each merchant sets their own reward rate per campaign. You can see the active rate on the merchant's campaign page before you make a purchase.

### 9. How do I redeem my points?

Go to the **Rewards** page, browse the available items, and click **Redeem** next to the one you want. Your points will be deducted and the reward will be confirmed on screen. You'll also receive a confirmation email.

### 10. Can my points expire?

Points do not expire under normal circumstances. However, if a merchant's campaign ends, any points tied specifically to that campaign may no longer be redeemable for campaign-specific perks. Your general point balance is always yours to keep.

### 11. What are Drops, and how do I claim one?

Drops are limited-time bonus rewards that merchants or the Nova Rewards team release to eligible users. When a Drop is available for your account, a notification will appear on your dashboard. Click **Claim** to add the bonus points to your balance. Each Drop can only be claimed once.

### 12. What is the referral bonus?

When you invite a friend using your personal referral link (found on the **Referral** page), and they sign up and complete their first qualifying action, both you and your friend receive a bonus. The bonus amount is set by the platform and shown on the referral page.

### 13. How do I find my referral link?

Go to the **Referral** page from the sidebar. Your unique link is displayed there — copy it and share it with friends via any channel you like.

## Wallet & Tokens

### 14. What is a Stellar wallet, and do I need one?

Nova Rewards uses the Stellar network to record and transfer your reward tokens securely. Think of a Stellar wallet as a secure digital pocket that holds your tokens. You need one to receive tokens directly on-chain. We recommend the free **Freighter** browser extension as the easiest option.

### 15. How do I connect my Stellar wallet?

1. Install the [Freighter](https://www.freighter.app/) browser extension.
2. Create or import a wallet inside Freighter.
3. On your Nova Rewards dashboard, click **Connect Wallet**.
4. Freighter will ask you to approve the connection — click **Approve**.

Your wallet address will then appear on your dashboard.

### 16. What is a "trustline" and why do I need to set one up?

Before your wallet can receive NOVA tokens, it needs to be told to accept them — this is called a trustline. It's a one-time setup step. On your dashboard, click the **Add NOVA Trustline** button. Freighter will show you a security confirmation — click **Approve**. You only need to do this once.

### 17. What is staking, and how does it work?

Staking means locking a portion of your NOVA tokens for a set period to earn extra yield (bonus tokens). Here's how it works:

1. Go to your dashboard and choose an amount to stake.
2. Your tokens are locked — they won't be spendable during the staking period.
3. While staked, your balance grows based on the current annual reward rate.
4. When you unstake, you receive your original tokens back plus the earned bonus.

The annual rate is set by the platform and shown before you confirm.

### 18. Can I unstake my tokens early?

Yes. You can unstake at any time. Your yield is calculated up to the moment you unstake, so you'll still receive any bonus earned so far — you just won't earn further yield after unstaking.

### 19. What does "swap for XLM" mean?

XLM is the native currency of the Stellar network. If you'd like to convert some of your NOVA tokens into XLM (for example, to cover small network fees), you can use the swap feature on your dashboard. You set the amount of NOVA to convert and a minimum amount of XLM you're willing to accept. If the exchange rate meets your minimum, the swap completes automatically.

### 20. Is my wallet safe? What security steps does Nova Rewards take?

Nova Rewards never stores your private wallet key — only you hold that through Freighter. All token transfers require your explicit approval via a digital signature in Freighter (a security confirmation pop-up). Admin functions on the platform use multi-approval controls, and all smart contracts have been independently audited. See our [Security Audit documentation](../audits/README.md) for details.

## Account Settings

### 21. How do I update my name or profile information?

Go to **Settings** from the sidebar. You can update your display name, bio, and linked Stellar wallet address there. Changes are saved immediately.

### 22. How do I change my email or password?

Email and password changes are handled on the **Settings** page under the **Security** section. You'll need to confirm your current password before making changes.

### 23. How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion from **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Your personal information will be anonymised within 30 days in line with our privacy policy. Your on-chain token balance is unaffected — tokens in your Stellar wallet remain yours.

### 24. I'm not receiving email notifications. What should I do?

Check your spam or junk folder first. If emails are going there, mark them as "Not Spam" to fix future delivery. You can also adjust your notification preferences in **Settings → Notifications**. If the problem persists, contact our support team.

### 25. How do I contact support?

Use the **Help** button in the bottom-right corner of any page, or email us at **support@novarewards.io**. We aim to respond within one business day.
