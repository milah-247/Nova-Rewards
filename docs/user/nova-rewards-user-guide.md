# Nova Rewards User Guide

Welcome to Nova Rewards! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started, from setting up your wallet to earning and redeeming rewards.

## 1. Setting Up Your Wallet (Freighter Install)

To use Nova Rewards, you'll need a Stellar-compatible wallet. We recommend using Freighter, a browser extension wallet.

1.  **Install Freighter**: 
    *   Go to the official Freighter website or your browser's extension store (Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons).
    *   Search for "Freighter Stellar Wallet" and add it to your browser.
    *   [SCREENSHOT: Freighter extension in browser store/after installation prompt]

2.  **Create or Import a Wallet**: 
    *   Once installed, click on the Freighter extension icon in your browser toolbar.
    *   Follow the on-screen prompts to either create a new Stellar wallet or import an existing one using your secret key or recovery phrase. **Always keep your secret key and recovery phrase secure and never share them with anyone.**
    *   [SCREENSHOT: Freighter wallet creation/import options]

3.  **Set a Password**: 
    *   You will be asked to set a password for your Freighter wallet. This password will protect your wallet on your device.
    *   [SCREENSHOT: Freighter password setup screen]

## 2. Connecting to Nova Rewards

Once your Freighter wallet is set up, you can connect it to the Nova Rewards platform.

1.  **Visit Nova Rewards**: 
    *   Open your web browser and navigate to the Nova Rewards platform (e.g., `https://app.novarewards.io`).
    *   [SCREENSHOT: Nova Rewards landing page]

2.  **Connect Wallet**: 
    *   Look for a "Connect Wallet" or "Login" button, usually located in the top right corner of the page.
    *   Click on it, and select "Freighter" from the list of wallet options.
    *   [SCREENSHOT: "Connect Wallet" button and Freighter selection]

3.  **Approve Connection**: 
    *   Your Freighter wallet will open, asking you to approve the connection to Nova Rewards. Review the details and click "Approve" or "Connect."
    *   [SCREENSHOT: Freighter connection approval pop-up]

After earning rewards, you can redeem them.

1.  **Go to Rewards/Wallet Section**:

2.  **Initiate Redemption**: 
    *   Select the tokens you wish to redeem and follow the prompts. You might need to specify a destination Stellar address if you're sending them out of the platform.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Here are answers to some common questions about Nova Rewards:

1.  **What is Nova Rewards?**
    *   Nova Rewards is a platform designed to incentivize user engagement through various campaigns, allowing users to earn and redeem digital tokens for their participation.

2.  **What is Freighter?**
    *   Freighter is a secure, open-source browser extension wallet for the Stellar network, allowing users to manage their Stellar assets and interact with decentralized applications.

3.  **Is Nova Rewards free to use?**
    *   While participating in campaigns to earn rewards is generally free, standard Stellar network transaction fees may apply for certain operations like redeeming tokens or staking, which are typically very low.

4.  **How do I earn rewards?**
    *   You earn rewards by participating in various campaigns listed on the platform. These can include completing tasks, surveys, engaging with content, or other specified activities.

5.  **What kind of rewards can I earn?**
    *   You will typically earn Nova tokens or other digital assets supported by the Nova Rewards platform, which can then be redeemed or staked.

6.  **How do I redeem my earned tokens?**
    *   You can redeem your tokens by navigating to the "My Rewards" or "Wallet" section, selecting the tokens you wish to redeem, and confirming the transaction through your connected Freighter wallet.

7.  **What is staking, and why should I do it?**
    *   Staking involves locking up your Nova tokens for a period to support the network's operations. In return, you typically earn additional tokens as a reward for your contribution.

8.  **Are my funds safe on Nova Rewards?**
    *   Nova Rewards utilizes a non-custodial wallet connection (like Freighter), meaning your tokens remain in your control within your wallet. Always be cautious of phishing attempts and ensure you are on the official Nova Rewards website.

9.  **What if I forget my Freighter wallet password?**
    *   If you forget your Freighter password, you will need your recovery phrase (seed phrase) to regain access to your wallet. Without it, your funds may be permanently inaccessible. Nova Rewards does not have access to your wallet password or recovery phrase.

10. **Where can I get more help or support?**
    *   For further assistance, please visit our help center, join our community channels (e.g., Discord, Telegram), or contact our support team through the platform's designated support portal.
