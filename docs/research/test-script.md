# Nova Rewards — Moderator Test Script

---

## Introduction (5 min)

> "Thanks for joining today. We're testing the Nova Rewards platform — not your abilities. There are no right or wrong answers. Please think out loud as you go. I may not answer questions during tasks, but I'll address them at the end. Do you consent to being recorded?"

---

## Background Questions (5 min)

- Walk me through how you typically interact with loyalty programs.
- Have you ever used a crypto wallet? Tell me about that experience.

---

## Tasks (35 min)

*Provide the staging URL before starting.*

### Task 1 — Connect Your Wallet
> "Imagine you just heard about Nova Rewards. Please connect your Freighter wallet to the platform."

- Watch for: confusion at wallet prompt, error handling, drop-off points

### Task 2 — Find a Reward Campaign
> "You want to earn rewards from a coffee brand. Find a campaign you'd participate in."

- Watch for: discoverability, filtering, clarity of campaign details

### Task 3 — Earn Tokens
> "Complete the action required to earn tokens from that campaign."

- Watch for: unclear CTAs, confirmation feedback, token balance update

### Task 4 — Redeem Tokens
> "You have tokens in your wallet. Redeem them for a discount."

- Watch for: redemption flow clarity, trust signals, success confirmation

### Task 5 — Create a Campaign *(merchants only)*
> "Set up a simple reward campaign for your business offering 10 tokens per purchase."

- Watch for: form complexity, terminology confusion, publish flow

---

## Debrief (10 min)

- What was the most confusing part of the experience?
- What worked well?
- How likely are you to use this? (1–10) Why?
- Anything else you'd like to share?
